How To Fold A 3D Star

These instructions will show you how to make a traditional origami 3D star. Stars a charming and symbolic form of paper folding that often associated with wishes, hope, and celebration. These tiny folded stars are not only a fun craft but also a meaningful gesture.

This model is simple and fun to fold, making it perfect for beginners. All you need is a strip of paper and a bit of patience.

3D Star Step-by-Step Instructions

·Difficulty: Easy

·Folding time: 10-15 minutes

·Paper: 15×15 cm

Step 1

Start with the white side of the paper facing up and fold in half horizontally.

Step 2

Fold the top left corner of the square down to the right edge, creating a triangle shape on the left side.

Step 3

Unfold the previous fold. Then, fold the top left corner of the square down to meet the right edge.
